Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jan 1, 2026

At Schedulizer ("we", "our", "us"), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we collect and use data when utilizing our Free Online Schedule Maker.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

Our scheduling application is built to prioritize user privacy. All schedule data, including tasks, durations, and calendar markers, are safely saved locally on your device via HTML5 Local Storage. We do not transmit or store your personal calendar events on external servers.

2. Analytics & Cookies

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) and advertising systems (such as Google AdSense) that deploy cookies to collect non-personally identifiable information. This helps us ensure the website functions correctly and display relevant advertisements.

3. Data Export

You retain complete control of your data. You may securely export your schedule via our PDF engine or JSON export mechanisms located in the Settings module.
